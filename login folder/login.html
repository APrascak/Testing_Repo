Mission Statement
“With Scrumble, we aim to pair like-minded individuals looking to improve their skills in an area of expertise. You will seamlessly be connected with either a mentor or mentee, based on your preference, and feel fulfillment as you provide guidance or gain important knowledge in your field of interest.”
