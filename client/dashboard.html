Home Page




Features

You can manage your profile information, change your preferences for matching, and change your status as a mentee and mentor. You may also view your profile and rating.

You can look up a list of available mentors, view their profiles, and send a request to add them.



You can view a list of users who want you to be their mentor, look at thier profiles, and either accept or decline their request.


You can look at the list of people who you are matched with, and if your relation to them is mentee, you can rate them as mentors when you view their profile
